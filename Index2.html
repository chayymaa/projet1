Healthy Eating Tips

Here are some simple tips to help you eat healthier:

  • Eat plenty of fruits and vegetables.
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains.
  • Limit processed foods and sugary drinks.
  • Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Balance your plate with protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats.

Healthy Recipes

Quinoa Salad

This refreshing quinoa salad is packed with protein and fiber.

Ingredients: quinoa, cucumbers, tomatoes, bell peppers, feta cheese, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, pepper.

Instructions: Cook quinoa according to package instructions. Chop vegetables and mix with cooked quinoa. Crumble feta cheese on top. Drizzle with lemon juice and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
